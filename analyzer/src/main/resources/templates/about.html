Text Complexity Analyzer

Main About

Text Complexity Analyzer - is a service where you can find out the syntactic complexity of your text.

This system can be useful for linguists, English teachers and students who are studying English. You can use it to determine the number of different syntactic and clausal complexity markers in your texts, and get a commentary based on their correlation

The following clausal and phrase complexity metrics are currently implemented:

Clausal:

  • Finite adverbial clauses
  • Wh-complement clauses
  • Verb+that clauses

Phrasal:

  • Nouns
  • Attributive adjectives
  • Premodifying nouns
  • Of-genitives
